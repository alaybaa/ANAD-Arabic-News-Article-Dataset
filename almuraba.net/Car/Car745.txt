e VSA survey calculates owner satisfaction in 45 specific areas related to a vehicle’s operation, comfort, safety and the overall purchase/lease experience. The 2005 ratings reflect input from buyers and lessees of new vehicles acquired from September 2004 through January 2005.The 2005 vehicle with the highest overall satisfaction in the survey was the Nissan Titan full-size pickup, while the highest rated car was the Nissan Maxima. Nissan vehicles won or tied for first place in seven categories.The only domestic product that was an outright winner in its category was the Lincoln Town Car, which placed atop the luxury/large car class. The Australian-built Pontiac GTO won the sporty car category, and the Chevrolet Equinox tied with the Hyundai Santa Fe at the top of midsize SUV.Top-rated sports car was the Porsche 911, while the leading luxury car was the Jaguar XJ.
